Limited Edition TAG Monoco sporting a calibre 36 automatic movement running at 36,000 vibrations per hour. Swiss-made C.O.S.C certified chronograph watch set within a 40.5mm black titanium carbide coated brushed steel case with an open display case-back revealing the intricacies of the TAG Heuer calibre 36 within. This iconic watch has a domed sapphire crystal with a double anti-reflection treatment, a black dial with chronograph displaying seconds at 9 o'clock and minutes at 3 o'clock by rotating systems, a date display at 6 o'clock and orange and blue vertical lines. The watch is secured by a fine alligator leather strap with orange stitching and a titanium deployment clasp. The watch is water resistant to 100 metres.
